Welcome to Mox's Deltarune Music Visualizer!

How to use:
  1. Click arrows to highlight leitmotifs.
  2. Click songs to see info.
  3. Drag songs around to rearrange them.
  4. Certain "dark mode" extensions may affect colors.
🗙

About

This is a fan-made visualizer for the music of Deltarune. Songs connected by arrows which signify leitmotifs or other melodies/patterns. It's currently a work in progress.

Disclaimers

Not all "leitmotifs" on this page are technically, actually leitmotifs. Some are just matching notes or even drum patterns. The consistent use of "leitmotif" is to make my life easier, sorry.

Many sources were referenced to make this project. Check out the Thanks section for links to resources that provide more detailed analysis.

Some choices in organizing or identifying leitmotifs can be subjective. This is my own attempt, so use your own judgement and compare resources before coming to conclusions.

Contact

If you find a mistake or have suggestions, feel free to reach out on Discord (@moxis) or Bluesky (coming soon).
